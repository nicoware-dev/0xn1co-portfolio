# Image Generation Prompts

Use these prompts with Gemini Nano (or other high-quality image generation models) to create consistent, high-fidelity hero images for the blog posts and portfolio projects.

**Global Style Guide:**
*   **Aesthetic:** "Deep Space" & Tech-Futuristic. Dark backgrounds, glassmorphism, neon accents.
*   **Colors:** Primary: Deep Royal Blue (`#3B82F6`), Cyan (`#0EA5E9`), Electric Violet (`#8B5CF6`). Backgrounds: Dark Navy/Black (`#020617`).
*   **Vibe:** Professional, sleek, modern, abstract representation of technical concepts.
*   **Medium:** 3D Render / Digital Art.
